## What validators do

Validators are the core participants responsible for checking and attesting to Safe transactions within Safenet. When a Safe transaction is proposed to the network, validators collectively evaluate it and produce a cryptographic attestation - a threshold signature - that serves as onchain proof of validation.

In Safenet Beta, validators run the FROST threshold signing protocol, meaning no single validator can produce a valid attestation alone. A quorum of validators must participate in each signing round.

## Who can be a validator in Beta

In Safenet Beta, the validator set is **permissioned** - validators are selected and onboarded by the Safe Ecosystem Foundation. This is a deliberate starting point to ensure stability and correctness before moving toward a permissionless model.

## Validator incentives

Validators earn SAFE token rewards in proportion to their stake and participation. Key mechanics:

- Rewards are distributed every two weeks
- Validators must maintain a minimum self-stake of **3,500,000 SAFE**
- Validators must meet a **75% participation threshold** to be eligible
- Validators earn a **5% commission** on rewards generated by delegated stake
